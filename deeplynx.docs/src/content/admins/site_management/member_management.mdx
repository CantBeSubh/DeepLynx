# Member Management

The Member Management tab of the Site Management page has a table containing all active and pending members.
**Site admins** can invite users, edit their details, and remove users from Nexus.
All users are placed in the INL organization as a default, but can be added to other organizations.

## Inviting Users

1. Navigate to the Member Management page by clicking the cog icon on the outtermost toolbar.
2. On the Member Management tab of the Site Management page, click the "Invite User" button.

<Image
  src="/docs/images/invite-site.png"
  alt="Invite User UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

3. Enter the user's email address and hit "Send Invitation".
4. The user will receive an email containing a link to join Nexus!

**Note:** Once a user accepts an email invitation their status will be switched to "Active".

<Image
  src="/docs/images/invite-save-site.png"
  alt="Invite User UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

## Editing Users

1. Click the pencil icon in the action column of the member management table.
2. Update the member's information in the Edit User modal and hit save.

<Image
  src="/docs/images/edit-user-site.png"
  alt="Edit User UI"
  width={800}
  height={400}
/>

## Removing Users

1. Click the trashcan icon in the action column of the member management table.
2. This will remove the user from Nexus.

**Note:** Once a user is removed from the organization, a new invitation is required to regain access.

  src="/docs/images/delete-user-site.png"
