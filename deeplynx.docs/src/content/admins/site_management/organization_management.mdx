# Organization Management

The Organizations tab of the Site Management page has a table containing all active organizations within Nexus.
**Site admins** can create, edit, and delete organizations.

## Creating Organizations

1. Navigate to the Site Management page by clicking the cog icon on the outtermost toolbar.
2. On the Organization Management tab of the Site Management page, click the "Organization" button.

<Image
  src="/docs/images/add-org-site.png"
  alt="Create Group UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

3. Enter the organization's name and description then click "Create".
4. The organization will be added to Nexus and displayed in the Organization Management table.

<Image
  src="/docs/images/save-org-site.png"
  alt="Save Group UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

## Editing Organizations

1. Click the pencil icon in the action column of the organization management table.
2. Update the organization's information in the Edit Organization modal and hit "Save".

<Image
  src="/docs/images/edit-org-site.png"
  alt="Save Edit Group UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

## Deleting Organizations

1. Click the trashcan icon in the action column of the organization management table.
2. This will remove the organization from Nexus.

**Note:** Once an organization is removed, it will be permanently archived.
A new organization must be created to regain access.

  src="/docs/images/delete-org-site.png"
