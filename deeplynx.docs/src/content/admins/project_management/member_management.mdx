# Member Management

The Users tab of the Project Management page has a table containing all active and pending members.
**Project admins** have the ability to add, edit, and remove users and groups from projects.

## Adding Members to Projects

1. Navigate to the Project Management page by clicking the slider bar icon on the inner toolbar.
2. On the Users tab of the Project Management page, click the "Add Group" or "Add User" button.

<Image
  src="/docs/images/add-member-proj.png"
  alt="Invite User UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

3. Select the members and their roles then click "Add to Project".
    - For Groups: select an existing group and role.
    - For Users: select the user's email address and role.

<Image
  src="/docs/images/add-group-proj.png"
  alt="Invite User UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

<Image
  src="/docs/images/add-user-proj.png"
  alt="Invite User UI"
  width={800}
  height={600}
/>

4. The new members will appear in the members table of the Project Management page!



## Editing Members in Projects

1. Click the pencil icon in the actions column of the member management table.
2. Update the member's information in the Edit Member modal and hit save.

## Removing Members from Projects

1. Click the trashcan icon in the action column of the member management table.
2. This will remove the member from the project.

**Note:** Once a member is removed from the project, they must be readded to regain access.
