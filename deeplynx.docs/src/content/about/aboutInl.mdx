# About Idaho National Laboratory

Welcome to Idaho National Laboratory (INL), America’s premier nuclear energy research and development laboratory. Established in 1949, INL has been at the forefront of innovative research, providing solutions to some of the nation's most pressing challenges in energy, security, and environmental sustainability.

## Our Mission

At INL, our mission is to discover, demonstrate, and secure innovative nuclear energy solutions, other clean energy options, and critical infrastructure. We strive to:

- Enhance the nation’s energy security by developing and applying advanced nuclear technologies.
- Foster clean energy solutions to mitigate environmental impacts.
- Protect and secure critical infrastructure, including cybersecurity initiatives.

## Our Research Areas

INL's research spans a wide array of fields. Our primary areas of focus include:

### Nuclear Energy

INL is a global leader in nuclear energy research and development. Our work includes:

- Advanced reactor design and testing.
- Nuclear fuel cycle research.
- Materials science and engineering.
- Safety and risk analysis.

### Renewable Energy

We are committed to advancing renewable energy technologies through:

- Bioenergy and biofuels.
- Geothermal energy.
- Energy storage solutions.
- Integration of renewable energy sources.

### National and Homeland Security

INL plays a vital role in ensuring the security of the nation's critical infrastructure. Our expertise includes:

- Cybersecurity.
- Nuclear nonproliferation.
- Defense systems and technology.
- Infrastructure resilience.

## Our Facilities

INL is home to some of the world's most advanced research facilities, including:

- **Advanced Test Reactor (ATR)**: A versatile research reactor used for materials testing and nuclear fuel research.
- **Materials and Fuels Complex (MFC)**: Specializes in nuclear fuel development and post-irradiation examination.
- **Energy Systems Laboratory (ESL)**: Focuses on energy efficiency, renewable energy, and grid integration.

## Our People

The strength of INL lies in its people. Our team comprises world-class scientists, engineers, and professionals dedicated to advancing the frontiers of science and technology. We foster a collaborative environment that encourages innovation and excellence.

## Community and Education

INL is committed to supporting our local and national communities through various outreach programs, including:

- STEM education initiatives.
- Public tours and educational events.
- Partnerships with universities and industry.

## Join Us

Whether you are a researcher, a student, or simply someone passionate about science and technology, we invite you to join us on our journey to create a better, more secure energy future. Together, we can make a difference.
